Summary: Latitude versus X

We studied weather data from over 500 weather stations across the world to understand the relation between Maximum Temperature, Cloudiness, Humidity and Wind Speed versus Latitude.

We plotted the data as Scatter Plots for each parameter to provide a visual representation that would readily illustrate whether or not there was an apparent variation with respect to Latitude. The example shown here is Maximum Temperature vs. Latitude.

We used MATPLOTLIB to obtain the plots. This site provides the visual summaries.
